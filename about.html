About Alumeet

Our Mission

Alumeet is an alumni association platform designed to help alumni stay connected, network, and collaborate. Our mission is to empower alumni relationships and foster a strong community.

Our History

Alumeet was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate alumni who recognized the need for a better way to stay connected with their alma mater and fellow graduates. Since then, we've grown to become a leading platform for alumni associations worldwide.
